Hello from Team Archineers! 🌞

Welcome to SolarVision, your one-stop solution for all things solar and shiny. We're here to guide you (and your solar panels) through any cosmic confusion you might have! ✨

What's this website about? Well, we've basically harnessed the power of the sun (and a pinch of cosmic good vibes) to build a fancy solar tracking system for educational buildings. 🏫
With the help of our amazing code, you can:

  • Peek at your Battery Voltage so you know how juiced up you are! 🔋
  • Check the Grid Return Voltage to see how much sunshine you're gifting back to the grid! ⚡
  • Monitor the Tilt & Azimuth Angles of your solar panels, ensuring they follow the brightest path in the sky. 🛰️
  • Watch Cost Savings go up, up, and away—like a rocket fueled by your leftover solar power! 💸
  • Easily switch between Grid or Battery mode with a single toggle. 🔌

All these superpowers are made possible by our snazzy code hooking into MQTT topics.
So, all the real-time data you see is plucked right from the cosmos of your hardware! 🛸

Where to start?

  • Dashboard: For your quick glances at the most important metrics. 📊
  • Analytics: For that in-depth, nerdy look at voltage trends—plus cool PDF reports to impress your friends. 📈
  • Support (You are here!): Because we love hearing from you, and we love help pages that are actually fun to read. 🙌

Whether you're an energy enthusiast, a school admin, or just a curious cat 🐱, we hope this website brightens your day just as much as the sun brightens our planet.
Now go forth and harness that sweet solar goodness! ☀️

If you have any more questions (or want to send us memes 😜), drop us a message at support@teamarchineers.io.
